The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild
Trial of the Sword
Normal Mode

LEGEND
•	Grab: Denotes when the runner must pick up weapons and other items
⚠	Backup: If something does not goes as planned, this denotes a strat to employ in an unfavorable situation
💀	Boss: The bosses of the trials
❗	Hard Floor: Denotes a floor with a degree of difficulty or randomness that makes it tough to complete quickly
🛡	Parry: Denotes a floor that requires at least one shield parry
🛏	Resting Area

---

Timing starts on Fade to Black after the cutscene following selecting Beginning Trials 

|- BEGINNING TRIALS -|

FLOOR 1 - BOKOBLIN CAMPFIRE
1 Bomb the Bokoblins
•	Grab Woodcutter’s Axe

FLOOR 2 – BOKOBLIN BASE
Bomb Fire ChuChu
Use 2 Bombs for Bokoblins
•	Grab Boko Bow
Break Crates → hopefully 10 arrows
⚠	If not 10 → grab arrows at base of Sentinel Bokoblin 
Arrow to Sentinel Bokoblins 

FLOOR 3 - CHUCHU AMBUSH
Throw Bomb to spawn point of right Fire ChuChu
Arrow Blue ChuChu
Detonate Bomb and Arrows to remaining ChuChus
Unequip Boko Bow 

FLOOR 4 - BOKOBLIN LOOKOUT ❗
Jump-drop Bomb to first Bokoblin
Bomb second Bokoblin
Bomb third Bokoblin
•	Grab Spiked Boko Bow
Break Crates → grab Arrows
Equip Stasis
Jump off Crates to Bullet Time Arrows to Blue Bokoblins 
⚠	If either Bokoblin survives, Stasis and Arrow
•	Grab Spiked Boko Bat 
•	Grab Soldier’s Broadsword
•	Grab Soldier’s Shield 
Arrows to 2 remaining Bokoblins 
Break Crates on top level 
•	6 Fire Arrows 

FLOOR 5 – GRASSY SKULL CAMP
Stasis Moblin → 2 Arrows to head
Equip Fire Arrows → Explosive Keg 
Throw Bomb into Skull 
Arrows to Moblin (Detonate Bomb after first Arrow)
Arrows to remaining enemies inside Skull Camp 

FLOOR 6 - STONE TALUS 💀
Equip Spiked Boko Bat 
Jump on Talus and Spinning Attack weakspot 
Throw Spiked Boko Bat if not already broken 
Equip Soldier’s Sword & Stasis

FLOOR 7 - RESTING AREA 1 🛏
⚠	Arrow pickups in Crates in right corner 

FLOOR 8 - LIZALFOS MARSH
Swim across water towards Lizalfos
Arrow to Electric Chu
Sword strikes to stunned Blue Lizalfos 1
Stasis Blue Lizalfos 2 → Sword strikes 
Equip Woodcutter’s Axe
Break Crates → Arrows
Equip Soldier’s Sword & Cryonis 
Sword strikes to Lizalfos
•	Grab Lizal Bow 
Equip Lizal Bow 
Arrow to Blue Lizalfos 
⚠	Fire Arrow Headshot will OHKO

FLOOR 9 - OCTOROK DOMAIN
Arrow to Water Octorok 
Cryonis → Shield Surf Jump off Cryonis → Unequip Shield → Arrows to Electric Wizzrobe
Arrow to Water Octorok 
Equip Bomb & use Bomb on Link
Equip Stasis

FLOOR 10 - LIZALFOS PLATFORM
Stasis Lizalfos 1 → Arrow Headshot
•	Grab Lizal Bow
Sneak up to left Lizalfos 2 → Sneakstrike → sneak to front → wait for Sneakstrike again
Sneak up to Lizalfos 3 → Sneakstrike → sneak to front → wait for Sneakstrike again 
Equip Woodcutter Axe & Cryonis
⚠	If you have less than 7 Arrows, Break Crate for Arrows 

FLOOR 11 - BOAT CRUISE ❗
Cryonis & Windbomb to Lookout
Bomb Bokoblins into water
Axe strikes to send Black Bokoblin & Moblin into the water below
Arrows to Sentinel Bokoblins
Unequip Woodcutter Axe & Equip Stasis

FLOOR 12 - BLUE HINOX 💀
Arrow to wake Hinox → Arrow to eye
•	Grab Knight’s Broadsword 
Sword strikes 
Arrow to eye
Sword strikes

Beginning Trials: COMPLETE 

---

BETWEEN TRIALS: 
Nothing → Begin Middle Trials

---
 
|- MIDDLE TRIALS -|

FLOOR 1 - BOKOBLIN UPDRAFT
Bomb both Bokoblins on left side
•	Grab Woodcutter’s Axe
•	Grab Boko Bow 
Break Barrels → Grab Arrows
Bullet Time Arrow Bokoblin → Unequip Boko Bow 
•	Grab Soldier’s Bow
Bullet Time Arrow remaining Bokoblin 
Break Barrels → Grab Arrows 

FLOOR 2 - WIZZROBE UPDRAFT
Bullet Time Arrow both Wizzrobes (WATCH THEM FALL!)
Arrow Bokoblin 
Break Crates → Grab Arrows
Bullet Time Arrow Bokoblins 

FLOOR 3 - UPDRAFT WARZONE
Glide to leftmost Bokoblin and Arrow
Break left Cates → 6 Ice Arrows
Glide to back Bokoblins and Arrow
•	Grab Reinforced Lizal Shield
Break Crates → 6 Bomb Arrows
Glide back toward platform & Arrow to remaining Bokoblins 
⚠	If you have 10 Bomb Arrows, equip Bomb Arrows and use on final blue Bokoblin
Unequip Bow

FLOOR 4 – DECAYED GUARDIAN (UPDRAFT) 🛡
Glide up to right platform
Parry Guardian Beam
Break Crates → Grab Arrows

FLOOR 5 - RESTING AREA 1 🛏
Open first chest → Falcon Bow 

FLOOR 6 - DARK TREE
Bomb Arrow at 3 Fire Keese in tree
Equip Arrows → Arrow Electric ChuChu
Whistle and Arrow Fire Keese 

FLOOR 7 - DARK SKULL CAMP
Arrow to drop lantern in Skull Camp
Equip Ice Arrows → fire at Fire Lizalfos 1 & 2
Equip Bomb Arrows → fire into Skull Camp 
⚠	If only 2 Ice Arrows, OHKO Lizalfos 1 and lure Lizalfos 2 into skull with Arrow

FLOOR 8 - NIGHTTIME BOKOBLINS
Bullet Time 3 Bomb Arrows at Bokoblins (aim all 3 at central Bokoblin)
•	Grab Dragonbone Boko Bat
Ice Arrow at Fire Wizzrobe
⚠	GRAB METEOR ROD IF NOT 6 BOMB ARROWS ON FLOOR 3

FLOOR 9 – DECAYED GUARDIAN (DARKNESS) 🛡
Climb right foundation
Parry Guardian Laser 
Break Crates → Grab Arrows

FLOOR 10 - BLACK HINOX 💀
Bomb Arrow/Meteor Rod at Hinox to burn away leg braces
Equip Arrows → fire at eye
Equip Dragonbone Boko Bat → catch on fire 
Spinning Attack 
Grab Thunderstorm Rod

FLOOR 11 - RESTING AREA 2 🛏

FLOOR 12 - GUARDIAN SCOUT POND
Arrows to all Guardian Scouts 

FLOOR 13 - GUARDIAN SCOUT MAZE
Throw Dragonbone Boko Bat at Guardian Scout 
•	Grab Guardian Sword
Whistle, Sword strikes to Guardian Scout II 1 & 2 
•	Grab Ancient Battle Axe
Equip Ancient Battle Axe

FLOOR 14 - GUARDIAN SCOUT POOLS
Arrow to 4 corner Guardian Scout 1s 
Wake Up one Guardian Scout 2 and move toward platform
2 Ice Arrows to other Guardian Scout 2 
Spinning attack Ancient Battle Axe Guardian Scout 2
Equip Thunderstorm Rod

FLOOR 15 - GUARDIAN SCOUT ARENA
Ice Arrow to left Guardian Scout 3
Run towards right Guardian Scout 3 shielding 
3 Thunderstorm Rod attacks → unequip and re-equip to skip cooldown → 3 Thunderstorm Rod attacks
Equip Ancient Battle Axe
Stasis Guardian Scout 3 and Spinning Attack
•	Grab & Equip Guardian Sword+

FLOOR 16 - GUARDIAN SCOUT IV 💀
Stasis enemy
Attack with Guardian Sword+
Equip Thunderstorm Rod and shock twice → Equip Guardian Sword and attack 4 times (repeat until Stasis refreshes)
Statis → Equip Guardian Sword+ and attack (repeat the last two steps until enemy is defeated)

Middle Trials: COMPLETE 
Windbomb to Sword Platform

---

BETWEEN TRIALS 
Eat Stamina Food & Attack Food
Begin Final Trials

-| FINAL TRIALS |-

FLOOR 1 - STALBOKOBLINS
Throw bomb at leftmost Stalbokoblin and Grab Spiked Boko Spear 
Spear remaining Stalbokoblins
Grab Spiked Boko Bat & Boko Bow
Break Crates and Grab Arrows

FLOOR 2 - STORMY SKULL CAMP
Whistle and Spear to Electric Keese 1
Throw Spear at Electric Keese 2 & Equip Boko Bat
Place Bomb by Explosive Keg 
Detonate when Cursed Lizalfos approaches 

FLOOR 3 - STALMOBLINS
Arrow Electric ChuChu 1 & 2
Break Barrels → Grab Arrows 
Approach edge on higher level to wake Stalmoblins
Bullet Time Arrows at all 5 
Unequip Spiked Boko Bow 
•	Grab Dragon Bone Boko Bow 
Equip Cryonis

FLOOR 4 - STALHORSEMEN ❗
Whistle & Cryonis ground water immediately to left 
Jump off Cryonis and Arrows to 5 approaching Stalbokoblins
•	Grab Dragonbone Boko Bat 
Run to central foundation → Whistle right before climbing
Arrows to approaching Stalbokoblins
Equip Stasis (Stasis and Arrow to remaining Stalbokoblins)
Break Crates → Grab Arrows & Shock Arrows
Equip Dragonbone Boko Bat

FLOOR 5 – STALNOX 💀
Stasis Stalnox & Arrow eye
Swinging Attack until broken 
⚠	If Stalnox does not butt stomp → Stasis & Arrow eye
Equip Spiked Boko Bat → Finish off eye
Grab Thunderblade, Flamespear, and Great Frostblade (Equip Great Frostblade if not picked up first)

FLOOR 6 - RESTING AREA I  🛏
Open first chest on right → Flamebreaker Boots
Pause and Equip Flamebreaker Boots
Open last chest on right → 3 Ancient Arrows 

FLOOR 7 - LAVA INLET
Arrow to Fire ChuChu
Bombs to Stone Pebblits & Rock Octorok 

FLOOR 8 - LIZALFOS SPIRES
OHKO Fire-Breath Lizalfos 
•	Grab Strengthened Lizal Bow 
OHKO Fire-Breath Lizalfos 
Break Crates → Grab Fire Arrows
OHKO Fire-Breath Lizalfos 

FLOOR 9 - FIERY SKULL CAMP
Arrow headshot Fire Wizzrobe & OHKO 
•	Grab Meteor Rod
Climb up to left Skull Camp eye → Arrow Moblin until dead & land with Sword Strike 
•	Grab Iron Sledgehammer
OHKO Fire-Breath Lizalfos 1 & 2 
•	Grab Reinforced Lizal Shield 

FLOOR 10 - LAVA LOOKOUT ❗
Equip Magnesis 
OHKO Fire-Breath Lizalfos 
Land on ground floor and magnesis chest out of lava → 3 Ancient Arrows 
Magnesis metal crate out of lava and whack Moblin 1 & 2
Break crate → Grab Fire Arrow 
Magnesis metal crate to whack Moblin 3
•	Grab Steel Lizal Bow
⚠	If Steel Lizal Bow is not easily retrievable, grab Royal Bow in Resting Area 3 instead

FLOOR 11 – IGNEO TALUS 💀
Wake Talus with Sword strike & jump on back 
Equip Iron Sledgehammer → Swinging Attack on weakspot
Equip Meteor Rod and Bombs

FLOOR 12 - RESTING AREA II   🛏
Open middle chest → Stone Smasher

FLOOR 13 - TUNDRA CAMPFIRE
Bomb Stone Pebblits
Equip Steel Lizal Bow (if picked up) and Arrow Ice ChuChu
Run up hill → jump off → Arrow to Bokoblins
Equip Fire Arrows 

FLOOR 14 - TUNDRA LOOKOUT
Fire Arrow to Ice-Breath Lizalfos
Meteor Rod at Ice ChuChus 
Shield Surf to icy chest → Fire Arrow to melt icy chest → 3 Ancient Arrows 
Shield-surf towards staircase
Equip Flamespear → strike Bokoblin 1
Fire Arrow to Bokoblin 2 
Spear strike to Bokoblin 3 
Equip Ancient Arrow & vaporize White Moblin
(Ancient Arrow Safety Strat: Sword strike White Moblin, grab/equip Dragonbone Moblin Club, Spinning Attack)

FLOOR 15 - TUNDRA RUINS
Bullet Time Fire Arrow at Ice Wizzrobe then Bokoblins 
Whistle → Equip Stone Smasher → Swinging Attack White Bokoblin
Equip Stasis → Moblin & Swinging Attack 
•	Grab Blizzard Rod 

FLOOR 16 - FROST TALUS 💀
Awaken Talus by running up close to it 
Meteor Rod at torso to stagger it 
Jump on back & Swinging Attack to weakpoint 
Equip Ancient Arrows & Meteor Rod

FLOOR 17 - WHITE LYNEL
Vaporize Lynel with Ancient Arrow

FLOOR 18 - RESTING AREA III 🛏
Grab Royal Bow from first chest if no Steel Lizal Bow

FLOOR 19 – DECAYED GUARDIANS 🛡 ❗
Advance at straight-ahead Guardian 1 & Parry laser 
Approach Guardian 2 to right → Equip Stone Smasher → Spinning Attack
Approach Guardian 3 → Position behind tree to avoid Guardian 4 laser → Parry Guardian 3
Approach Guardian 5 → Parry Guardian 4 laser
Spinning attack to Guardian 5
Approach Guardian 6 → Parry laser 
Equip Meteor Rod 

FLOOR 20 - GUARDIAN STALKER
Approach Stasis Guardian with Whistle Sprint
Stasis Guardian Stalker & OHKO Ancient Arrow 

FLOOR 21 - GUARDIAN SKYWATCHER
Meteor Rod grass to create updraft → Approach Skywatcher → OHKO Ancient Arrow 

FLOOR 22 - GUARDIAN UNIT
Meteor Rod grass to create updraft → Approach Guardian Skywatcher → OHKO Ancient Arrow 
OHKO Ancient Arrow Guardian Stalker 
Meteor Rod grass to create draft → OHKO Ancient Arrow Guardian Turret
⚠	If miss, climb tower and Spinning Attack with Stone Smasher, stun with Arrow if needed
⚠	If Ancient Arrow Safety Strat is used, use leftover Ancient Arrow for second chance
Equip Blizzard Rod

FLOOR 23 - THE KITCHEN SINK 💀
Statis Lynel & vaporize with Ancient Arrow
Blizzard Rod approaching Bokoblins 
Bullet Time OHKO Ancient Arrow Guardian Turret 

Final Trials: COMPLETE
Windbomb to middle platform and run to draw Master Sword
Timing ends on A Press to Pull.
